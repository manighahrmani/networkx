To do this, I have constructed minimum elimination orderings of these graphs. Recall that we can construct a minimum triangulation from a minimum elimination ordering with the following algorithm:
- Let G_0 = G (the input graph)
- Let G_i be the graph obtained from G_{i-1} after the elimination of the ith vertex in the elimination ordering.
- Let G_n be the empty graph
- Let H, the minimum triangulation, be the graph obtained from G by the addition of all the edges during the minimum elimination ordering.

For example if 10505 is eliminated in 5x5 grid, then 10405 is in madj(10504) or vice versa.
